Live Data
Data sources & methodology — please read before using figures

Live (World Bank API): project name, ID, country, region, sector, total commitment, IBRD/IDA split, approval & closing dates, lending instrument, borrower, and development objective (PDO). These are fetched in real time from the World Bank Projects API.

Modeled / illustrative: disbursement %, lending spread, arrears, quarterly cashflows, and repayment schedules are not published per-project by the API. They are generated deterministically from each project's commitment and dates to demonstrate the dashboard's analytics layer. Do not treat these as actual World Bank financial figures. Wire them to your internal SAP / Redshift loan views to make them authoritative.
